DR MRS VANDERTRAMP

A mnemonic for French verbs using être in the passé composé.

Acronym French Verb English Translation
D Devenir to become
R Revenir to come back
M Monter to go up, to get on
R Rester to stay
S Sortir to go out, to leave
V Venir to come
A Aller to go
N Naître to be born
D Descendre to go down, to get off
E Entrer to enter
R Rentrer to return, to go home
T Tomber to fall
R Retourner to return, to go back
A Arriver to arrive
M Mourir to die
P Partir to leave

A helpful mnemonic for French language learners.
