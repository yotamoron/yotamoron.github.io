    Formation Rules

    Verbs ending in -ER

    Drop the -er and add -é.

    Example: parler → parlé

    Verbs ending in -IR

    Drop the -ir and add -i.

    Example: finir → fini

    Irregular Verbs

    These verbs have unique endings that must be memorized.

    Examples:
    avoir → eu
    être → été
    faire → fait
    prendre → pris
